**Prerequisites:**  Node.js


1. Install dependencies:
   `npm install`
2. Set the `GEMINI_API_KEY` in [.env.local](.env.local) to your Gemini API key
3. Run the app:
   `npm run dev`

# eTopia: The Digital Civilization Platform

## Vision

**eTopia** is a self-sustaining digital ecosystem that transforms underserved communities into connected, empowered, and economically resilient societies through technology, social innovation, entrepreneurship, and collective action.

Its mission is simple:

**"Connect People. Enable Purpose. Solve Global Challenges."**

Unlike traditional platforms that focus only on connectivity or social networking, eTopia combines digital inclusion, workforce development, social entrepreneurship, community governance, and impact financing into one integrated platform.

---

## The Core Problem

Millions of people remain excluded from opportunities because they lack:

* Access to digital jobs
* Access to innovation ecosystems
* Community support structures
* Skills and mentorship
* Funding and investment
* Decision-making tools
* Mechanisms to address local and global challenges

Most solutions address only one part of the problem.

eTopia creates an ecosystem that addresses all of them together.

---

# Platform Architecture

At the center sits the **eTopia Core Engine**.

It is governed by:

### Principles

* Integrity
* Transparency
* Inclusiveness
* Sustainability
* Leave No One Behind

---

# Track 1: Commercial Track

The Commercial Track generates economic value that sustains the ecosystem.

### ComeShine

A digital talent marketplace connecting:

* Freelancers
* Students
* Professionals
* SMEs
* NGOs

with global opportunities.

Services include:

* Software development
* AI services
* Data labeling
* Design services
* Remote support
* Digital transformation consulting

### ComAct

A project execution hub where organizations can outsource ICT projects to certified eTopia teams.

Benefits:

* Job creation
* Skill development
* Revenue generation
* Local economic growth

### Innovation Incubators

Support for:

* Startups
* Social enterprises
* Technology innovators

Services:

* Mentorship
* Co-working spaces
* Business acceleration
* Market access

Revenue generated here funds the Social Track.

---

# Track 2: Social Track

The Social Track focuses on societal transformation.

## I-Decide Framework

An AI-assisted decision-support system helping citizens make informed choices about:

* Education
* Employment
* Health
* Environment
* Community development

The framework promotes responsible participation and evidence-based decisions.

### Community Help Centers

Physical and virtual support centers providing:

* Digital literacy
* Technology access
* Mentorship
* Career guidance
* Legal and civic information

### Behavioral Navigation System

An AI-powered guidance layer that promotes:

* Ethical behavior
* Positive online engagement
* Responsible technology use
* Community well-being

### Community Code of Conduct

Establishes:

* Trust
* Safety
* Accountability
* Respectful collaboration

# Resource Engine

eTopia uses a hybrid sustainability model.

Resources come from:

### Sponsorship

Corporate partners fund initiatives aligned with ESG and social impact goals.

### Donations

Individuals and organizations contribute to specific causes.

### Volunteers

Experts donate skills and mentorship.

### Investments

Impact investors support scalable community solutions.

### Revenue

Commercial Track activities continuously generate funding.

This creates a self-reinforcing ecosystem rather than a grant-dependent project.
